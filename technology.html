01 — Advanced Sensors

A laboratory
on your wrist

SmartWatch integrates eight distinct sensors, always capturing health data even in low-power states.

  • Optical Heart Rate — green and infrared LEDs track blood volume changes continuously
  • Electrical Heart Sensor — ECG-grade readings from stainless steel electrodes
  • SpO2 Sensor — measures blood oxygen saturation
  • Skin Temperature Sensor — tracks nighttime variation trends
  • Barometric Altimeter — tracks floors climbed and elevation gain
  • Tri-Band GPS — GPS plus GLONASS plus Galileo for accurate positioning anywhere
  • 6-Axis IMU — accelerometer plus gyroscope for motion and fall detection
  • Ambient Light Sensor — auto-adjusts display brightness
02 — Battery Efficiency

Charge less.
Live more.

The Pear P2 chip delivers 36 hours of all-day battery life while keeping all sensors fully active.

  • 36 hours standard use — Always-On Display and all sensors active
  • 60 hours Extended Mode — reduces background features for maximum duration
  • Fast charge: 15 minutes gives you 8 hours of use
  • Full charge from 0 percent in approximately 55 minutes

03 — Smartphone Connectivity

Paired perfectly.
Works everywhere.

SmartWatch works fully with both iOS and Android via Bluetooth 5.3 and Wi-Fi 6.

  • Full iOS and Android compatibility — one watch, any smartphone
  • Bluetooth 5.3 for fast, energy-efficient pairing up to 30 meters
  • Wi-Fi 6 for home network sync and over-the-air app updates
  • NFC — contactless PearPay payments with a double-tap gesture
  • LTE cellular option — make calls without your phone nearby
  • End-to-end encrypted health data — never sold or shared
