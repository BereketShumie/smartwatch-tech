Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers

Does SmartWatch work with Android phones? +
Yes. SmartWatch is built for full compatibility with both iOS and Android. Download the free PearOS companion app from the App Store or Google Play. It works with any iPhone running iOS 16 or later, and any Android phone running Android 10 or later.
How accurate is the health monitoring? +
SmartWatch optical heart rate has been validated to within 2 BPM at rest and 5 BPM during moderate exercise. Blood oxygen accuracy is within 2 percent of clinical pulse oximeters. SmartWatch health features are for general wellness awareness and are not intended to replace professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.
Can I swim with SmartWatch? +
Yes. SmartWatch is rated WR50 — water resistant to 50 meters. It features swim workout modes with lane lap counting, stroke type detection, SWOLF score, and automatic Water Eject mode to clear the speaker after your swim. It is safe for pools, open water, and the shower.
How long does the battery actually last? +
In standard usage with Always-On Display enabled you will get approximately 36 hours — more than enough for a full day plus overnight sleep tracking. Extended Battery Mode pushes that to 60 hours. PearCharge fast charging gives you 8 hours of use from just 15 minutes on the dock, and a full charge takes about 55 minutes.
Is my health data private and secure? +
Yes. All health data is processed on-device by the Pear P2 chip wherever possible — it never has to leave your watch to generate insights. Data synced to PearCloud is end-to-end encrypted in transit and at rest. Pear Technologies does not sell your personal or health data to any third parties under any circumstances. You can export or permanently delete all your data at any time in the PearOS app under Privacy Settings.
