Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers

Does SmartWatch work with Android phones?+
Yes. SmartWatch works with both iOS and Android. Download the free PearOS app from the App Store or Google Play. Compatible with iOS 16 or later and Android 10 or later.
How accurate is the health monitoring?+
Heart rate is validated to within 2 BPM at rest and 5 BPM during moderate exercise. SpO2 accuracy is within 2 percent of clinical pulse oximeters. SmartWatch health features are for general wellness awareness and are not a replacement for professional medical advice.
Can I swim with SmartWatch?+
Yes. SmartWatch is rated WR50 — water resistant to 50 meters. It has swim workout modes with lap counting, stroke detection, and automatic Water Eject after your swim. Safe for pools, open water, and the shower.
How long does the battery last?+
Up to 36 hours in standard use. Extended Battery Mode pushes that to 60 hours. Fast charging gives you 8 hours of use from just 15 minutes on the dock, and a full charge takes about 55 minutes.
Is my health data private and secure?+
Yes. All health data is processed on-device wherever possible. Data synced to PearCloud is end-to-end encrypted. Pear Technologies never sells your data to third parties. You can export or delete all your data at any time in the PearOS app under Privacy Settings.
