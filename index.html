Sensors & Health Monitoring

A laboratory on
your wrist

SmartWatch integrates eight distinct sensors, processed by a dedicated health co-processor that runs independently of the main P2 chip — so health data is always being captured, even when the watch is in low-power mode.

📡

Optical HR

Green and infrared LEDs detect blood volume changes to derive continuous heart rate.

⚡

Electrical Sensor

Stainless electrodes on the case generate ECG-grade electrical heart readings.

🫁

SpO2 Sensor

Red and infrared light measure blood oxygen saturation levels on demand and in background.

🌡️

Temperature

Dual-sensor array measures wrist surface temp to track nighttime variation trends.

🧭

Barometric Altimeter

Always-on altimeter tracks floors climbed, elevation gain on hikes, and air pressure.

📍

Tri-Band GPS

GPS + GLONASS + Galileo for highly accurate positioning in any region and environment.

📳

Haptic Engine

Advanced taptic feedback with 12 distinct patterns for nuanced, meaningful alerts.

🔊

Ambient Light

Automatically adjusts display brightness for perfect readability — indoors or in sunlight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers

Does SmartWatch work with Android phones?+
Yes! Unlike many competitors, SmartWatch is built for full compatibility with both iOS and Android. Simply download the PearOS app from the App Store or Google Play to set up and manage your watch.
How accurate is the health monitoring?+
SmartWatch's optical heart rate sensor has been validated in peer-reviewed studies to within ±2 BPM at rest and ±5 BPM during moderate exercise. The ECG app is cleared by the FDA for detecting atrial fibrillation. SpO2 accuracy is ±2% compared to clinical pulse oximeters.
Can I swim with SmartWatch?+
Absolutely. SmartWatch is rated WR50 — water resistant to 50 meters. It features a full swim workout mode with lane lap counting, stroke type detection (freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke), SWOLF score, and automatic water eject to clear the speaker after your session.
How long does the battery actually last?+
In standard usage with the Always-On Display enabled, you'll get around 36 hours — enough for a full day and then some. With Extended Battery Mode (which reduces some features), you can push to 60 hours. A 15-minute charge on PearCharge gives you 8 hours of use.
Is my health data private and secure?+
Yes. All health data is processed on-device by the Pear P2 chip where possible. Data synced to PearCloud is end-to-end encrypted. PearTech never sells your health data to third parties. You can delete all your data from PearCloud at any time in your account settings.
What band sizes and styles are available?+
SmartWatch comes in 41mm and 45mm cases. Bands are available in Small/Medium and Medium/Large fits. Official styles include Fluoroelastomer Sport, Braided Nylon, Genuine Leather, Stainless Milanese Loop, and the lightweight Solo Loop. Third-party bands using the standard quick-release connector are also widely available.
Does SmartWatch include GPS without a phone?+
Yes — all SmartWatch models include built-in tri-band GPS (GPS + GLONASS + Galileo). You can go for a run, hike, or bike ride and get full route mapping and distance tracking without your phone anywhere nearby.
