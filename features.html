01 — Health Monitoring

Your health,
always aware

SmartWatch monitors what matters most with sensors that rival dedicated medical devices.

Heart Rate Tracking
  • Continuous optical heart rate — every second, all day and night
  • ECG-grade electrical sensor — detect irregular rhythms from your wrist
  • Resting heart rate trends tracked and graphed over time
  • High and low heart rate notifications for your safety
Activity Tracking
  • Step counter with daily goals and streak tracking
  • Calories burned — active and total throughout the day
  • Floors climbed via barometric altimeter
  • Sedentary alerts — reminds you to stand and move every hour
Sleep Monitoring
  • Sleep staging — light, deep, and REM phases tracked automatically
  • Morning sleep score and readiness index
  • Smart alarm — wakes you at the lightest sleep phase
  • Overnight SpO2 and respiratory rate monitoring
02 — Fitness Tracking

Train smarter,
not just harder

Whether it is a morning run, a swim session, or a HIIT class, SmartWatch tracks every rep, step, and stroke.

Workout Tracking
  • 70+ workout types — running, cycling, swimming, yoga, strength, and more
  • Tri-band GPS for precise outdoor route and distance tracking
  • Real-time pace, cadence, and heart rate zone coaching
  • Automatic workout detection — SmartWatch starts recording automatically
  • Post-workout recovery time and readiness score
Step Counter
  • Continuous step counting with daily goal tracking
  • Weekly step trends and monthly progress reports
  • Compete with friends via shared step challenges
Performance Insights
  • VO2 Max estimation — track cardio fitness improvements over time
  • Training load and recovery balance recommendations
  • Route history — view and compare past workouts on a map
03 — Smart Notifications

Stay connected.
Stay present.

SmartWatch delivers the right information at the right moment — a quiet haptic tap on your wrist.

Message Alerts
  • SMS and all major messaging apps surfaced directly on your wrist
  • Quick replies by voice or preset short responses
  • Smart notification filtering — only people you prioritize break through
Call Notifications
  • See who is calling without reaching for your phone
  • Answer or reject calls directly from your wrist on the LTE model
  • Built-in microphone and speaker for wrist calls
Calendar Reminders
  • Upcoming meeting alerts with 5-minute and 1-minute warnings
  • Traffic-aware departure notifications
  • Daily agenda view directly on your watch face
04 — Watch Faces and Battery

Personal and powerful

Custom Watch Faces

  • 200+ pre-designed faces — analog, digital, and hybrid styles
  • Complications for weather, fitness rings, and heart rate at a glance
  • Photo watch faces — set any photo as your backdrop
  • Quick-swap bands — Silicone, Leather, Braided Nylon, Milanese Loop

Battery and Water Resistance
