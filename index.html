Why SmartWatch

SmartWatch by Pear Technologies combines precision health sensors, fitness coaching, and seamless smartphone connectivity into an elegant wearable you will never want to take off.

Health Monitoring

Continuous heart rate, blood oxygen, ECG rhythm detection, and stress tracking around the clock.

Fitness Tracking

70+ workout modes, GPS route mapping, and smart coaching that adapts to your performance level.

Smart Notifications

Messages, calls, and calendar alerts delivered silently to your wrist — you choose what matters.

Long Battery Life

Up to 36 hours of all-day use, with fast charging that gives you 8 hours from just 15 minutes.

Product Design

Precision in
every detail

Crafted from aerospace-grade aluminum and sapphire crystal glass, SmartWatch is as durable as it is beautiful. Available in 41mm and 45mm case sizes to fit any wrist.

  • Always-On AMOLED display — bright in direct sunlight
  • Sapphire crystal glass — scratch and crack resistant
  • Titanium, Aluminum, and Ceramic case options
  • WR50 water resistance — safe for swimming and showers
  • Quick-swap band system — hundreds of styles available
